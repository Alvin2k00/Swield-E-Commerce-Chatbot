SWIELD STORE - CUSTOMER SUPPORT Q&A BOOKLET
==========================================

--- GENERAL ---

Q: What is SWIELD?
A: SWIELD is an online store offering electronics, clothing and home essentials. We provide fast delivery and flexible membership plans designed for every lifestyle.

Q: What does SWIELD sell?
A: SWIELD sells products across three main categories: Electronics (laptops, phones, audio, accessories), Clothing (everyday basics, seasonal styles, curated collections) and Home Essentials (kitchen, bedding, storage).

Q: How do I contact SWIELD customer support?
A: You can reach our customer support team through the chat assistant on our website, by emailing support@swield.com or by calling our helpline Monday to Friday 9am to 6pm.

Q: Is SWIELD available in my country?
A: SWIELD currently delivers across the United Kingdom. International shipping is coming soon.

Q: Is SWIELD a trustworthy store?
A: Yes, SWIELD is a trusted online retailer. All payments are secured and encrypted. We have a clear returns policy and dedicated customer support.

--- MEMBERSHIP PLANS ---

Q: What membership plans does SWIELD offer?
A: SWIELD offers three membership plans. Free at no cost per month, Standard at £7 per month and Premium at £15 per month. Each plan comes with different benefits to suit your shopping needs.

Q: What does the Free plan include?
A: The Free plan gives you access to all product categories, standard delivery in 5 to 7 days and basic customer support. It costs nothing and is great for occasional shoppers.

Q: What does the Standard plan include?
A: The Standard plan costs £7 per month and includes everything in the Free plan plus fast delivery in 2 to 3 days, 10 percent off all orders and priority customer support.

Q: What does the Premium plan include?
A: The Premium plan costs £15 per month and includes everything in the Standard plan plus free next-day delivery, 20 percent off all orders, early access to new arrivals and a dedicated account manager.

Q: Which membership plan is most popular?
A: The Standard plan is our most popular plan. It offers a great balance of savings and fast delivery at just £7 per month.

Q: Can I upgrade my membership plan?
A: Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. The new plan takes effect immediately and you will be charged the difference.

Q: Can I downgrade my membership plan?
A: Yes, you can downgrade your plan at any time. The change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Q: Can I cancel my membership?
A: Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees. Your benefits continue until the end of your current billing period.

Q: Is there a free trial for paid plans?
A: Yes, new customers can try the Standard or Premium plan free for 14 days. No credit card is required to start the trial.

Q: How do I sign up for a membership?
A: You can sign up by clicking the Sign In button on the top right of our website and creating an account. You can then choose your preferred membership plan.

--- ORDERS ---

Q: How do I place an order?
A: Browse our categories, add items to your cart and proceed to checkout. You will need an account to complete your purchase.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Once your order is dispatched you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also track your order from the My Orders section in your account.

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: You can cancel an order within 1 hour of placing it. After that the order may already be processed. Contact our support team as soon as possible if you need to cancel.

Q: I have not received my order. What should I do?
A: We are sorry to hear that. First check your tracking link to see the latest update. If the tracking shows delivered but you have not received it, please contact our support team within 7 days and we will investigate immediately.

Q: My order arrived damaged. What do I do?
A: We sincerely apologise for that. Please take a photo of the damaged item and contact our support team within 48 hours. We will arrange a replacement or full refund right away.

Q: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?
A: Address changes are possible within 1 hour of placing the order. After that please contact support and we will do our best to help.

--- DELIVERY ---

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Delivery times depend on your membership plan. Free members receive standard delivery in 5 to 7 days. Standard members receive fast delivery in 2 to 3 days. Premium members receive free next-day delivery.

Q: How much does delivery cost?
A: Free members pay standard delivery charges at checkout. Standard members receive discounted delivery. Premium members receive free next-day delivery on all orders.

Q: Do you offer next-day delivery?
A: Yes, next-day delivery is available. Premium members receive it free on all orders. Other members can select next-day delivery at checkout for an additional fee.

Q: Do you deliver on weekends?
A: Yes, we deliver on Saturdays. Sunday delivery is available in selected areas. You will see available delivery slots at checkout.

--- RETURNS AND REFUNDS ---

Q: What is the SWIELD returns policy?
A: You can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Some exceptions apply for hygiene products and personalised items.

Q: How do I return an item?
A: Log into your account, go to My Orders, select the item you want to return and follow the return instructions. We will email you a prepaid returns label.

Q: How long does a refund take?
A: Refunds are processed within 3 to 5 business days after we receive your returned item. The amount will be credited back to your original payment method.

Q: Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?
A: Yes, exchanges are available for clothing and home essentials. Select the exchange option when initiating a return and choose the replacement item.

Q: I was charged incorrectly. What should I do?
A: We apologise for that inconvenience. Please contact our support team with your order number and we will review the charge and issue a correction within 24 hours.

--- PRODUCTS ---

Q: What electronics does SWIELD sell?
A: Our electronics range includes laptops, smartphones, headphones, earbuds, smart speakers, phone accessories, cables and the latest tech gadgets. We stock over 120 products in this category.

Q: What clothing does SWIELD sell?
A: Our clothing range includes everyday basics like t-shirts and jeans, seasonal collections for spring, summer, autumn and winter, and curated style edits for men and women. We stock over 200 products.

Q: What home essentials does SWIELD sell?
A: Our home essentials range includes kitchen tools and cookware, bedding and pillows, storage solutions, cleaning supplies and home organisation products. We stock over 90 products.

Q: Are SWIELD products covered by warranty?
A: Electronics come with a minimum 12 month manufacturer warranty. Clothing and home essentials are covered by our 30 day returns policy. Extended warranties are available for selected electronics at checkout.

Q: Does SWIELD sell branded products?
A: Yes, SWIELD stocks a range of well known brands alongside our own SWIELD label products which offer great quality at lower prices.

Q: Are products in stock?
A: Stock levels are shown on each product page. If an item is out of stock you can sign up for a restock notification and we will email you when it is available again.

--- ACCOUNT ---

Q: How do I create a SWIELD account?
A: Click the Sign In button on the top right of the website and select Create Account. Enter your name, email address and a password to get started.

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: Click Sign In and then Forgot Password. Enter your email address and we will send you a reset link within a few minutes.

Q: How do I update my personal details?
A: Log into your account and go to Account Settings to update your name, email, address or password.

Q: How do I delete my account?
A: You can request account deletion from Account Settings under the Privacy section. Please note this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

--- PAYMENTS ---

Q: What payment methods does SWIELD accept?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely.

Q: Is my payment information safe?
A: Yes, all payments are encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways. SWIELD does not store your full card details.

Q: Can I pay in instalments?
A: Yes, we offer buy now pay later options through Klarna and Clearpay at checkout for eligible orders.
